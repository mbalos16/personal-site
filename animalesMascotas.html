Animals with Their Pets

This project was a collaborative effort between a Spanish pets association called "Fundacion Brego", the photographer Amada Egle and myself as a graphic designer. The goal of this project was to create a beneficial calendar called "Animals with Their Pets" to raise funds for the association and support their efforts to provide food for the animals in their care.

Role: As a Graphic Designer, my role in this project was focused on the visual aspect of the printed calendars. I was responsible for selecting typography, arranging content, and placing design elements in a visually appealing and user-friendly manner.

The Project: For this calendar, we decided to include Spanish holidays and festive days, highlighting significant occasions to celebrate pets and remind people of their importance throughout the year. We also incorporated a minimalistic moon calendar for astronomy enthusiasts and paid tribute to influential women in history.

We printed a total of 131 calendars at a local printer in Madrid, Spain, and sold them for 10€ each. The production cost of each calendar was 7.85€, resulting in a total earning of 281.65€ for the association after the campaign.
