Textile


On these little projects, I was working on making design more accessible to people working with commonly-used tools.

First of all, I had to create something with Adobe Illustrator that I would love to print and after that, print it on transfer paper with an inkjet printer. Then, I had to iron the printed paper onto a t-shirt or a tote bag so as to have a unique and personalised design.

Transfer paper: PPD - Inject T-Shirt Transfer Paper
Printer: Canon MG5750
T-Shirt & Tote bag: Wordans Needen.
