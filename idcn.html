IDCN NEWSLETTER


International Dual Career Network (IDCN) is an association of international organisations and corporations facilitating the integration of mobile employee partners through awareness, guidance, and professional networking.

I was part of this association during an eight-month contract as a volunteer and I was involved in the design of the trimestral newsletter for the IDCN Luxembourg team. These Newsletters were designed with PowerPoint, as per the association rules and in accordance with the association’s branding.
