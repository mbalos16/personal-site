XIBALBÁ

Book cover inspired by Mayan mythology, accompanied by an extensive research process on this ancient civilization.

The design process for this project unfolded as follows:

  • Analysis of the problem to be addressed in the project
  • Research on pre-Columbian culture
  • Selection of the specific Mayan culture to focus on
  • In-depth research on the Mayans
  • Creation of sketches on paper and digitally
  • Refinement of the chosen sketch towards the final design
  • Selection of a suitable color palette and finalizing the book's design
  • Creation of an animated GIF
  • Development of the project dossier
  • Final review and revisions
