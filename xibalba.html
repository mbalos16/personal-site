XIBALBÁ



Book cover based on Mayan mythology, including an extensive investigation process on that civilisation.

The design process for the creation of this project has been as follows:

  • Analysis of the problem to be dealt with in this project
  • Research on pre-Columbian culture
  • Choosing the culture on which to focus the project
  • Research on the Mayans
  • Sketching on paper and digital
  • Perfecting the sketch to the final result
  • Color palette and finishing the book design
  • Creation of the animated Gif
  • Creation of the dossier
  • Final review.
