BEER LABELS


Who doesn´t love beer? For me, having a beer means time with my lovers one, hot summer and an amazing Sun. That's why I consider the beer labs so importants for the user.

On this project, I've designed a full set of labels for the bottles of a microbrewery. During this project, I was involved in the beer-making process to understand customer preferences and adapting the designs to the brewery needs.

On the labels you can find a little description of the process for each one of this labels.

ENJOY!
BOARD GAMES

Like you could read in the description, a beer is spending time with friends, and this was the main purpose of this label. This was a Pilsner Beer, with a gold-yellow colour and a strong hops flavour.

CHIRIPA

This was one of my first labels and is a representation of Luxembourg city, being this the city where the beer was made.

COV-IPA

One beer made during the Covid-19 situation. On this label, you can see a representation of that period, with hope and hop.

HOWTH

San Patricio is one of the most famous Irish beer parties. One of his representation is clover and that's why it is also the design for this label. This beer was named according to one of the Irland island, Howth.

ZAR

This beer at the beginning was created with more alcohol to survive the delivery to the Imperial Court of Czar. His name came from "tsar" which means "king". The first one to have this title was Iván IV.
